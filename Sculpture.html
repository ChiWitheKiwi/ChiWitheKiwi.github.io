Self Portrait

I made a bust of my own head with water based clay using reference shots of my head from 8 angles. Then glazed and fired for longevity. (2023)

Lady with a Sword

This piece was done while studying a model with a water based clay. Creative liberties were taken with the facial features, the weapon, the skull under the foot, and the seat. Then glazed and fired for longevity. (2023)

Red Man

Custom Action Figure (Broly)
