Glitch in the Meaning

A glitch edit of an image using the audio software, Audacity. The focus is a photo of "The Ecstacy of Saint Teresa", by Gian Lorenzo Bernini, one of the most impressive sculptures in my eyes. I felt as though the glitch aesthetic adds a thought provoking justaposition to the traditional marble sculpture. (2020)

Augmented Reality

For this piece, I hand drew each frame while considering distance, creating the illusion of depth. The animation is left not to loop, as to continue the story that they came and have left, leaving cracks in the wall and floor. Created in Adobe Animate and Adobe Aero. (2020)
